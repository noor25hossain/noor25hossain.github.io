About Noor Research Academy

Noor Research Academy for Business & Accounting is dedicated to supporting Master’s and PhD students in business, accounting, and related disciplines. The Academy provides structured research guidance, academic training, and scholarly resources to help students develop strong research skills, complete high‑quality theses, and meet international academic standards.

Founded by a PhD scholar, the Academy bridges the gap between theory and research practice with a strong emphasis on methodology, academic writing, data analysis, and publication‑focused research.

Our Mission

To empower Master’s and PhD students in Business and Accounting with high‑quality research guidance, academic training, and scholarly resources that promote research excellence, integrity, and global academic standards.

Our Vision

To become a trusted international research academy recognized for advancing excellence in business and accounting research, supporting postgraduate scholars, and contributing to high‑impact research.

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence – Commitment to rigorous, high-quality research standards
  • Integrity & Ethics – Promoting ethical research practices
  • Scholarly Support – Student‑centered mentoring and guidance
  • Critical Thinking – Encouraging deep analytical skills
  • Global Perspective – Aligning research with international standards

Research Ethics & Academic Integrity

Noor Research Academy maintains the highest standards of integrity and ethical research practice. We strictly discourage plagiarism, ghostwriting, or any unethical academic behavior. Our focus is on mentoring, guidance, and skill‑building.
